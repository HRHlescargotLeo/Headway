HEADWAY — INTERACTIVE WIREFRAMES (mid-fidelity, monochrome)

HOW TO OPEN
1. If you received a .zip: right-click it and choose "Extract All" first.
   (Opening a page directly from inside the zip can strip its styling.)
2. Open  index.html  in any browser.

Every page is now fully self-contained (styles + scripts are built in),
so each page also renders correctly on its own.

WHAT TO TRY
- Top-right of each page: "Annotations: On/Off" toggles the dashed
  requirement callouts (for design hand-off, switch them Off).
- Accessibility bar: larger text, high contrast, readable font, read aloud.
- Filters that actually work: Brain Injury & Me, Find local support
  (postcode + tabs + map pins), News, Information library, Training,
  Glossary A–Z, Search, Events.
- Donate: single/monthly, amounts, Gift Aid, multi-step flow.

These are end-user journeys only (no admin/editor screens), greyscale by
design so a visual/brand layer can sit on top.
